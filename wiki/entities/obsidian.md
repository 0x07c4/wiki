# Obsidian

## Role

Obsidian is the local markdown workspace used to browse, inspect, and navigate the wiki while the LLM maintains files.

## Relevance

In the seed note, Obsidian functions like the human-facing IDE for the knowledge base:

- the user reads and inspects pages
- graph view reveals structure and orphan pages
- plugins such as Dataview and Marp can extend how the wiki is explored

## Relationships

- supports the browsing layer for the [LLM Wiki](../concepts/llm-wiki.md)
- complements the [Ingest, Query, Lint](../concepts/ingest-query-lint.md) workflow
